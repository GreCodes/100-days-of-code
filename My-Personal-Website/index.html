Why Aventurera Del Sur?

So, I was in The Netherlands, stranded. I lost my passport on a short trip to Amsterdam. I noticed 2 days after, and I was in complete shock! I had to travel to Berlin soon to start a new chapter in my life. My new passport with a German visa.
I was lucky to have free accommodation in a huge house though, and while I waited more than a month for this passport, I improvised melodies and a song was born : " Aventurera del Sur, les digo que la vida, la vida es una cancion". I jotted it down and I recorded it on my computer. I still have to ask my friend Robinson, to lend me a hand to record itthe way it should be. By then, October 2014, I was thinking about all the adventures I have lived in the countries I have visited and stayed.
A song conforted me.

I am certain, travellers and expats will identify with the intensity of experiences lived abroad. I guess sometimes we feel like in our own movie.

When a close one leaves this earth

Talking about death is not a taboo for me. At 27, I was about to leave this world due to an internal hemorrhage. One of my ovaries was profusedly bleeding and my toraxic cavity was filling up with blood. And this took place just out of the blue.

I survived.

After that experience, I am always aware, your most love ones can just leave this earthly world in the blink of an eye

Young, old, rich, poor, death can always visit you

Now, imagine that delicate moment. A family member passes and you are in another continent, with a weeks old baby and no money for a flight ticket.

That was me in 2019, when my father died from an aortic aneurysm.

I know he would have said Stay there with your family" , but the guilt was eating me inside.

I stayed.

One of the very few friends I have attended his funeral, and my cousin took a message.

One of the reasons, and the most important we move to Denmark, was that we would be closer to the family.

And even though farfar or grandpa doesn't live in Copenhagen, we often visit him.

He will leave us very soon

How can I explain my daughter when he is gone?

How do I tell her "Luna, he is not home anymore, we cannot visit him and go for a walk again

Sometimes I regret not starting younger having children, so that their grandparents would have more time with them

And we should love each other and show it every day
